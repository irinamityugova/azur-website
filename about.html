Why free?

We are focused on the mission. As a non-profit, we improve economy, let creatives create, fight for human rights, work on solving global problems, and more. Money is the energy that follows gratitude. We do our part - provide the value. You can support the mission with your donation.

Innovation

We are developing a platform that instantly generates the top three teams that match your project's needs. Currently, volunteer recruitment and project matchmaking is done manually. As we integrate automations and language processing models, we will be pleased to announce Azur Agency App that will bring our administrative tasks to lightspeed. We'd rather focus on most important tasks - fulfilling dreams and making an impact.

Securing the Future

We will ensure that your project is built with secure solutions, so you can confidently support your creative(s). If you are happy with our work, please share about your experience and consider donating. We feed "starving artists", so they can continue to deliver excellent work.
