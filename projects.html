Open Projects

As a volunteer, you contribute to real world projects and develop meanigful solutions for real clients, whether they are other global nonprofits or individual self-starters. As creatives, we bring ideas to reality with the best skillsets we got. Many projects require collaboration, so we use the Discord platform to talk about most projects and stay up to date with the progress.

As a client, you can create a thread in Discord's #projects chat with a complete description of tasks that need to be done and @mention any skills you'd like help from. You may get immediate interest in your project or keep the window open for a couple weeks for the right people to find your project.
