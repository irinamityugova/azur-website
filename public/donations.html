Thank You For Your Support

We are a registered 501c3 non-profit organization and run completely on donations. If you are happy with our services and want to help us, and support our mission, please contact us to learn how you can donate today!

This donation will help a lot to achieve our mission. We'll be able to :

  • Support families of creatives in financial struggle.
  • Power the global economy by educating creatives and applying their talent and skills in real-world projects, helping small businesses, self-starters, and other creatives.
  • Transform the world's mindset to givers and doers.

If you would like to contribute further, contact us to become a volunteer, or talk to your friends about our cause!
