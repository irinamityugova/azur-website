Volunteers

We welcome creatives around the globe.

Because we are better together, we enjoy crafting the most impactful solutions and supporting one another in achieving dreams and career goals.

Evolving technology, shifts in demands of employers, and growing competition kept many creatives focused on a task to "stand out". Companies have analysts, developers, designers, etc. Who do you have?

Why not have each other?

We promise to continuously evolve to provide the most value for active creatives.

We are excited to start working with you! Acceptance-based recruitment system allows us to welcome anyone from anywhere. Your friends and connections are welcome to volunteer as long as they can communicate, be kind to others, and have a desire to contribute. We have no age or experience barriers. Azur is a transparent, open, supportive community on a mission to be the world's greatest nonprofit leader and serve billions of people by more doing and less talking. Company Description

Azur Agency is a non-profit organization that helps people bring their ideas to reality. We market, create, illustrate, and develop creative solutions that help others. We value diversity and collaborate to develop skills and independence amongst our team members. We believe that diversity can not only unify perspectives but also provide an advantage in delivering impactful creative solutions.

Role Description

This is a remote volunteer (unpaid) role at Azur Agency. As a volunteer, you will work on diverse projects that may require a range of skills. You will propose contributions to tasks that match your skills, interests, and career goals. You will have the opportunity to collaborate with teammates to develop your skills and contribute to real-world projects.

This role requires volunteers to be proactive, propose specific contributions, manage time commitment, stay up to date with project's tasks, and take charge of personal accomplishments. We usually don't have a manager to assign us specific tasks, but some projects may allow for a team lead who would help you navigate the progress. If no team lead is assigned, you are welcomed to lead the project to its completion.

Qualifications

  • Applicants under 18 must receive school and parental consent.
  • No education or experience is required.
  • Be respectful of others' differences and skill levels.
  • Flexible and adaptable with the ability to take constructive feedback. Passion for community development and improvement.
  • Web development, graphic design, social media, creative writing, art, video editing, photography, or other creative skill is a plus, but not mandatory.
  • Experience with project management software such as Trello or Asana is a plus. Experience with non-profit organizations is a plus
